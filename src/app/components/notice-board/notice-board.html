VFive Elysium 1 - Community Rules

General Rules

  • Visitors: Please inform security in advance for visitors arriving at odd hours. After 10 p.m., visitors will be let inside only after confirmation from the resident.
  • No Littering: Do not litter the premises and absolutely no throwing garbage from windows or balconies.
  • Parking: Residents must park in designated parking slots. Do not park in driveways or any other place that obstructs vehicle movement.
  • Driving: No rash driving inside the compound.
  • Secure Doors/Windows: Ensure all doors and windows are securely closed when entering or exiting the premises.
  • Smoking: Strictly no smoking in common areas.

Waste & Disposal

  • Waste Segregation: Please segregate wet and dry waste. Place garbage bags outside around 10 a.m. in a bucket to prevent leaks.
    • Paper and cardboard together
    • All types of plastics together
    • Napkins and similar items together
    • Food waste separately

    Important: The cleaning staff will not collect waste from flats where these categories are mixed.

  • Disposal Rules: DO NOT throw condoms, sanitary pads, or other bulkier items into flushes, bathroom, or kitchen sinks. This will block the STP. Please deposit these items with the dry waste, covered or packed in paper, or use the incinerator (available from 10 a.m. to 11 a.m.).

Facilities & Deliveries

  • Swimming Pool: The pool is open from 6 a.m. to 8:30 p.m. Collect the key from the security cabin and return it after use. All rules displayed near the pool must be strictly followed.
  • Rooftop: The rooftop is locked by the caretaker when he leaves. Please collect the key from the security cabin to access it after it is locked.
  • Food Delivery: Door-step food delivery is allowed only until 10 p.m. After 10 p.m., residents must collect their food from the main gate.
  • Parcel Delivery: There is no door-step delivery for parcels (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.). Residents are required to collect their goods from the reception area.
